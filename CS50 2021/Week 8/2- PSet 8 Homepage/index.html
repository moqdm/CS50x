About county:
"Chabahar is the largest ocean port in Iran, the third petrochemical hub."

Chabahar County (Persian: شهرستان چابهار‎) is a county in Sistan and Baluchestan province, Iran. Capital of the county is Chabahar and Population of county is around 291,910 With population density of 36 people per sq km. Distance from Chabahar to Tehran is 1,120 mi.

Why should travel to Chabahar?

In addition to its commercial location, Chabahar has many historical and cultural attractions. The climate of this city and the surrounding areas is always spring and temperate, and therefore it is called Chahar Bahar (Four springs). Chabahar isn't only rich in culture and history, It's also a natural museum of unique geomorphological phenomena that multiplies the importance of preserving and introducing this region.
