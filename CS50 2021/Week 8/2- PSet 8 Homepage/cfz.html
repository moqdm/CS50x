About Chabahar F.Z:

Chabahar Free Zone (CFZ) founded in 1992 to use for the development of the country, creation of employment and representation in global markets which has been allocated area of 167,712 acres for that. CFZ achieved its importance for obtaining the shortest and safest way for Central Asia landlocked countries to warm waters and its accessibility to the largest mineral, natural gas and petroleum resources of the world and as the only ocean port of the country.
